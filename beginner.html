Kingdom Of Heaven

King Baldwin

In the film Kingdom of Heaven, King Baldwin IV (played by Edward Norton) is the noble, masked "Leper King" of Jerusalem who rules with wisdom and strength despite his decaying body. He is depicted as a respected, selfless leader holding a fragile peace against Saladin, ultimately acting as a tragic heroic figure. He wears a silver mask to conceal the disfigurement caused by leprosy. Portrayed as intelligent, honorable, and deeply respected, holding power through sheer willpower rather than physical strength.
